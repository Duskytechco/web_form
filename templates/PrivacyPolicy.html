Information We Collect

At Motosing, we highly value your privacy and are committed to providing you with a personalized and efficient experience. When you engage with our services, we may gather a comprehensive set of personal details to ensure that we can serve you effectively. This includes essential personal information such as your name, NRIC (National Registration Identity Card), phone number, email address, title, gender, race, marital status, Bumiputera status (whether you are Bumiputera or non-Bumiputera), residential address (including the number of years you have been residing there), and your ownership status (whether you own, rent, or have another arrangement).

Additionally, we collect product-related details, including brand, model, product type, and the condition of the product (whether it's new, used, or reconditioned). We also gather extra information, such as how you learned about our services, the best time for us to contact you, and your motorcycle license information.

To facilitate transactions, we require banking information, including the name of your bank, bank account number, and the type of account you hold. Furthermore, we collect employment-related information, such as your employment status, the nature of your company, company name, company phone number, company address, your start date with the company, net salary, gross salary, EPF (Employees Provident Fund) contribution, and your salary term (whether you are paid monthly, annually, or by another arrangement).

Lastly, we may ask for reference contacts, including the names of your references, their NRICs, the nature of your relationship with them, and whether you co-reside with them.

We want to reassure you that your data's confidentiality and security are paramount to us. Our Privacy Policy provides comprehensive information on how we handle and protect your data. If you ever have questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to contact us at admin@motosing.com. Your trust and satisfaction are our primary concerns.
